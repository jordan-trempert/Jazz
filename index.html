- About Jazz -

Jazz Securo is a professional award-winning announcer and actor. He's introduced fighters, musicians, athletes, sports teams, and entertainers in major arenas around the world. He's announced events on PPV, cable, network, and satellite TV. Seen on ESPN, NBC, NBC Sports Network, CBS Sports Network, AXStv to name a few. Jazz has won "Ring Announcer of the Year" in multiple regions. His dynamic talent and professionalism have put him at the top of his field.
